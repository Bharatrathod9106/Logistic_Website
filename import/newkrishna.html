About Us

We are the company of experienced people, providing the best service in the field of Courier & Cargo, with a great belief in customer satisfaction. We specialize in providing courier & cargo service for U.K., U.S.A., U.A.E, and Kenya. Our services include Door to Door Courier, Door to Airport Cargo, Airport to Airport Cargo and LCL/FCL Freight Cargo.

We are also an agent of DHL, FedEx, Skynet and Skycom (U.K., U.S.A., U.A.E., & Africa Self Network).We are the team of dedicated people, and we work hard to maintain our reputation for being a trustworthy & timely service provider.
